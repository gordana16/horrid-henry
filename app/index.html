Game Rules

  • For each turn, players can move from one to three boxes (horizontally or vertically) before ending their turn.
  • If players pass over a box containing a weapon, they leave their current weapon on site and replace it with the new one.
  • If players cross over adjacent squares (horizontally or vertically), a battle begins
  • The player can choose to attack or defend against the next shot
  • As soon as the life points of players falls to 0, they lose. A message appears and the game is over
